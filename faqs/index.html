FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is The Simulation™?

The Simulation™ is an ambitious, story-focused VR game currently in development. It offers a full-length narrative experience, placing you in the midst of a brutal war between Muffins and Donuts. As a test subject, you will navigate various challenges and influence the outcome of the conflict, all while being immersed in a rich, engaging storyline.

When will The Simulation™ be released?

The Simulation™ is still in the development phase and is a long ways away from being completed due to its ambitious scope. We are dedicated to creating a high-quality, comprehensive game experience, which requires significant time and effort. We appreciate your patience as we continue to develop this unique VR adventure.

How can I get involved in the development of The Simulation™?

As of the time of this writing, we are still in the pre-production phase of making the main game, so we are not looking for new team members just yet. However, if you're interested in working on this project in the future, please feel free to reach out and leave your contact information. We will keep your details on file and contact you when we begin looking for additional team members.
