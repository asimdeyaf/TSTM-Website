ABOUT

The Simulation™ is a VR game currently in development. This game is about a conflict between Muffins and Donuts, with players taking on the role of a test subject that finds themselves intimately involved in this war.

This website serves as a resource for developers working on the project, providing necessary information related to the game's development process.

If you are not a developer, you are still welcome to look around. Just be warned of story spoilers.
